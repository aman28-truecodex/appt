Organization Structure
Mission

Mission is an brief statement that defines why the organization exists and it describe the fundamental purpose of the Organization, especially what it provides to customers and clients

Vision

Sets out the compelling rationale (strategic soundness) for the firm’s direction. Uses distinctive and specific language to set the firm apart from its rivals.

Values

Organizational values guide your organization’s thinking and actions. You can think of your organizational values in terms of pro social, market, financial and achievement.
